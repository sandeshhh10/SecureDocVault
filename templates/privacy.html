Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 July 2026
SecureDoc is an educational security-research project that demonstrates honeyword-based intrusion detection and encrypted document storage. Please do not upload genuinely sensitive personal, financial, or medical documents.

This Privacy Policy explains what information SecureDoc ("we", "the service") collects, how it is used, and how it is protected when you use the application. By creating an account and using the service, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account credentials. A username you choose and your password. Your password is never stored in plaintext — it is processed with PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256 (200,000 iterations) and stored only as a salted hash within a honeyword set.
  • Documents you upload. Files you place in your vault are encrypted with AES-256-GCM before they are written to storage. We hold only the encrypted ciphertext, along with the file name, size, and last-modified time needed to display your vault.
  • Session data. A signed session cookie that keeps you logged in. It is cryptographically signed to prevent tampering and does not contain your password.
  • Security & audit records. To protect accounts, we log security-relevant events such as suspicious or failed login attempts, account lockouts, and intrusion-detection triggers. These records may include a timestamp, IP address, and browser user-agent string.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your document vault.
  • To store, list, encrypt, decrypt, and delete the documents you upload.
  • To detect and respond to unauthorised access — including brute-force lockout after repeated failed logins and honeyword-based intrusion detection.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the reliability of the service.

3. How Your Data Is Protected

Documents are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM, and passwords are stored only as salted PBKDF2 hashes. The service applies additional protections including account lockout after repeated failed attempts and a decoy-vault intrusion-detection system. No security measure is perfect, but plaintext passwords and unencrypted documents are never persisted.

4. Cookies

We use a single, essential session cookie to keep you signed in. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking or analytics cookies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data or share it with third parties for marketing. Encrypted data may be stored using infrastructure providers (for example, a managed Postgres database and application hosting) solely to operate the service. Those providers only ever hold opaque, encrypted content.

6. Data Retention

Your account data and encrypted documents are retained while your account is active. Security and audit logs are retained for as long as needed to protect the service. When a document is deleted from your vault, its stored ciphertext is removed.

7. Your Choices

  • You can add or delete documents in your vault at any time.
  • You can stop using the service and request removal of your account data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data can be directed to the SecureDoc project maintainers.
